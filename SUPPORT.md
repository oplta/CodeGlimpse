## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Why doesn't the syntax highlighting appear?**
A: Sometimes macOS Quick Look caches need a reset. Try running `qlmanage -r` in Terminal or restarting your Mac.

**Q: Is my code safe?**
A: Yes. CodeGlimpse runs 100% offline and sandboxed. Your code never leaves your computer.

**Q: Which languages are supported?**
A: We support over 17 languages including Swift, Python, JavaScript, Go, Rust, Ruby, C++, and many more.
